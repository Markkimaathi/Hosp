Welcome to My Health Connector

My Health Connector is a free service that helps you find health and social care services in your local area. We provide information on a wide range of services, including:

Counselling and Guidance

Get help with mental health issues, relationship problems, and more.

Community & Aged Care

Find support for older people, people with disabilities, and their families.

Crisis & Emergency Support

Get immediate help in a crisis situation.

Transport

Get help with getting to and from appointments.

Financial Support

Get help with managing your finances.

Volunteering and Employment

Find opportunities to give back to your community or find a job.

Health and Well-being

Get help with staying healthy and well.

Support Group

Connect with others who understand what you're going through.
