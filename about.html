WHO WE ARE

We are dedicated to providing accessible and healthcare information to elderly living in rural areas. Our goal is to empower these communities by ensuring they have the knowledge and resources needed to make informed health decisions and improve their overall well being.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible and reliable healthcare information to elderly individuals in rural areas, empowering them to make informed health decisions and improve their quality of life.

Our Vision

We ensure a future where elderly person, regardless of their location, has access to the healthcare information and resources they need to lead a healthy and fulfilling life.

Our Story

Health Connector was founded to address lack of accessible healthcare information in rural communities. Inspired by the challenges faced by elderly individuals in remote areas, we aim to bridge the gap between these populations and essential healthcare services.
